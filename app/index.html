Love Your Place. Find Your Way.

The Nature School of Philadelphia provides an environment that encourages a child’s innate curiosity to grow and flourish. Exploration of the natural world is core to the Nature School’s philosophy of learning.

Our holistic approach to education will nurture the head, heart and hands of students to awaken and inspire critical thinking, emotional intelligence and artistic expression - preparing students to step into their authentic selves, moving forward into the world to contribute in a meaningful way.

Our Mission

To cultivate a culture of environmentalism through deep nature connection and understanding in the Philadelphia region.

Our Vision

To re-nature Philadelphia by interconnecting children and their families within their local, national and global communities.
